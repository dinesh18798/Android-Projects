Griddle: a Grid-Based Word Game

Griddle: the simple but addictive word game that offers a fine balance of skill and luck.  You'll be dealt 25 letters from a specially designed deck with 74 letter cards to be placed in turn on the grid.  Score as many points as you can.  Words are checked against a dictionary very similar to the official Scrabble (TM) word list, so you'll also learn some bizarre words that you would never have believed were words in the process!

This is the free version: coming soon will be a paid version that will offer a 2-player mode: play against a friend or against the built in AI.

Score as follows:

Length Points
2 1
3 3
4 7
5 10

Make words going across or down: sub-words count, so try to make 5-letter words that have lots of shorter words within them.  The maximum score for a 5-letter word (including its sub-words) is 37 points (and yes, there are many words in the list worth 37).

Example word: ACIDS

Words:

 ACIDS (10) + ACID (7) + IDS (3) + ID (1)

Score for ACIDS = 21

This is an early release of the game: bare bones but fun. Enjoy!!!
